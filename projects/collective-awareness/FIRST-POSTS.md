**Slide 4:** "This means: debt = money creation. The entire economy runs on debt. By design."
**Slide 5:** "The question isn't whether this is true — it's why nobody taught you this in school. 🔍"

### X Thread:
```
Where does money actually come from? 🧵

Most people assume banks lend out deposits from other customers.

They don't.

When a bank gives you a loan, it literally creates new money. Out of nothing. This is how 97% of all money in circulation is created.

It's called fractional reserve banking, and it's not a conspiracy — it's in the Bank of England's own publications.

So the entire economy runs on debt. By design. If all debts were repaid, there would be almost no money left.

Makes you wonder who designed it, and who benefits.

🔍 Source: Bank of England Quarterly Bulletin, 2014
```
